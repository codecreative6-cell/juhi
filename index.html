About

Her voice carries the warmth of jasmine and the weight of dusk — a melody that lingers long after the music fades.

Juhi is a fabulous singer whose music bridges the intimate and the infinite. Rooted in the rich tradition of Indian classical music, her voice moves effortlessly between the meditative and the joyful — from the sacred raga to the tender moment of a heartfelt song. She sings not just with her voice, but with her whole being. Every note a small act of devotion. Every performance a shared breath with the audience.

The Art of Juhi
